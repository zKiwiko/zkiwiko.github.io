Lunar Overview

Lunar is a Cronus Zen Script made for 'Call of Duty: Black Ops 6'. The script is designed to be nearly Plug and Play, with some Adjusting required. Some adjustments are necassary as not every user would share the same values, or would want specific ones for their play style.

You can view specific information about the script by pressing the buttons below.

Opening And Closing

There are 2 Menus associated with Lunar. The Main Menu and Weapons Menu. To enter the Main Menu, you can press and hold LT/L2, then tap the Pause button on your controller.

To enter the Weapons Menu, you can press and hold LT/L2, then tap the View/Share button.

To exit and save; While not editing a Weapon or Mods Values, press the Home button associated with your console.
I.e; The XBOX button on Xbox, PS Button on PS4/5, or the Switch Home button.

TL;DR:
LT/L2 + Menu/Pause -> Opens Main Menu.
LT/L2 + View/Share -> Opens Weapon Menu.
Xbox/PS Button -> Close and Save.

Scrolling & Navigation

Scrolling through the Main or Weapons menu is the same as every other Zen Script.

Left Dpad -> Scroll Left through mods, weapons, or values.
Right Dpad -> Scroll Right through mods, weapons, or values.

A/X to enter a sub menu or edit a mods values.
B/Circle to Backout of the respective part.

Main Menu

The Main Menu consists of a few Sub Menus.
Sensitivity, HIP Mods, ADS Mods, Fire Mods, Settings. (In order)

Weapons Menu

The weapons menu holds all available weapons in Black Ops 6. Each weapon has default Anti Recoil values - made for the gun with zero attachments.
You can fine tune the respective weapons Anti Recoil that youre hovering over by press A/X.

A Positive Vert value will make the gun pull more down.
A Negative Vert value will make the gun pull more up.

A Positive Hori value will make the gun pull more to the right
A Negative Hori value will make the gun pull more to the left.

Fine tuning may become necassary when using certain attachment combinations, but most of the time its not needed.

To select a weapon. Press Y/Triangle when hovering over a weapon to make it your primary, And X/Square to make it your secondary.

You will know a weapon is your Primary or Secondary by the LED of your Cronus Zen being Yellow (primary), or Blue (Secondary).

Overview

The Sensitivity mod affects your ingame sens. How fast your turn rate is ingame by limiting your sticks output. And can also affect your Anti Recoil. Lower values may require Anti Recoil Fine Tuning.

I.e: THIS AFFECTS HOW FAST YOU AIM. YOU DO NOT PLUG IN YOUR INGAME SENS.

Recommended Values: HIP = 100, ADS = 75 || HIP = 80, ADS = 40

In-Game Requirements.

Vertical Sensitivity: 20 Horizontal Sensitivity: 20 ADS Modififer: 1.00 Stick Curve: Standard Right Stick Deadzone Min: 5 Right Stick Deadzone Max: 99 Left Stick Deadzone Min: 5 Left Stick Deadzone Max: 99

Hip Sens

The HIP value of the Sensitivity Mod affects your Right Stick Sensitivity when not aiming.
Min Value = 0, Max Value = 100.

Ads Sens

The ADS value of the Sensitivity Mod affects your Right Stick Sensitivity when you are holding your AIM button.
Min value = 0. Max Value = 100.

Slide Cancel

Values: Delay
Activation: Sprinting, Pressing Crouch.

The Slide Cancel mod will press your jump button after the specified Delay Value in ms.
The higher the value, the longer itll take to press Jump.

For this mod to work, you must be Sprinting, with your left stick toward its outer edge - Then pressing Crouch to slide.

Auto Sprint

Values: Delay
Activation: Pushing Your Movement Stick Towards the edge.

The Auto Sprint Mod will automatically double tab your Movement Stick when you push it towards the edge.
It will do so after the specified Delay Value in ms.

Bunny Hop

Values: None
Activation: Holding Jump.

The Bunny Hop mod will spam your jump button until it is released.

YY Spam

Values: Speed Activation: Double Tap Sprint.

The YY Spam mod will press your Swap button 4 times. With the Speed value determining the delay between the button presses.

Rapid Melee

Values: None
Activation: Holding Melee

The Rapid Melee mod will spam your Melee button, as long as its held down.

Aim Assist

Values: Radius, Strength, Hip Radius, Hip Strength.

The Aim Assist Mod will spin your Aim Stick in a Circle.
The size of which is determined by the Radius Value. The Speed of which determined by the Strength Value.

When Aiming, the Radius and Strength Values will be used. When not aiming, the Hip Radius and Hip Strength will be used.

Hold Breath

Values: Delay
Activation: Pressing Aim

The Hold Breath mod will press your Sprint button (Focus), after the specified Delay Value.
Which in turn will make your sniper rifle focus.

This mod will only activate when a sniper is equipped as your current weapon. I.e: LW3A1, SVD, LR 7.62

Ping On Fire

Values: Delay
Activation: Aim & Fire

The Ping On Fire mod will press the Ping Button after the specified Delay Value in ms.
To run the mod, you must hold Aim and press Shoot to ping.

Drop Shot

Values: Delay
Activation: Aim & Fire

The Drop Shot Mod will press and hold the Crouch button to drop shot, after the Delay Value in ms.
To run the mod, you must hold Aim and press Shoot to drop shot.

This mod will not run if you are using a Sniper Rifle.

Crouch Spam

Values: Speed
Activation: Aim & Fire

The Crouch Spam mod will spam your crouch button when you are shooting. The speed of which determined by the Speed value in ms.

To run the mod, you must hold Aim and press Shoot to crouch spam.

Deadzone

The Deadzone mod will change your sticks deadzone. You can choose a value of 0 to 100.

Layouts

You can use any controller layout with Lunar. The only exceptions are swapping your triggers and bumpers and southpaw aiming.
Other than that, all button layout options in Black Ops: 6 are supported. Just simply choose the one you use and play.
